Different delicious foods and their definitions

Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings baked in an oven.

Burger

A burger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty placed inside a sliced bun, often served with lettuce, tomato, and cheese.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice combined with seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.

Pasta

Pasta is an Italian food made from wheat dough mixed with water or eggs and formed into different shapes.
